Drive Controls:
    Left Stick - driving
    Right stick - rotation
    Aim and Shoot - Left Trigger
    Digital Intake - Right Bumper
    Reset Forward Direction - B
    Zero Hood - Left Middle Button

Setup:
    Enable Robot
    Rotate Robot to face forward
    Reset Forward Direction (Hit B)
    Immediately after, Zero Hood (Hit Left Middle Button)
    Hit up on the D-pad
    Test Limelight (Tap Left Trigger) 
        Limelight will turn off or flash
    Re-Zero Hood
